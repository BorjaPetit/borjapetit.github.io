WELCOME

I'm a PhD candidate in Economics at CEMFI since September 2015, and will defend my thesis in July 2019.

RESEARCH INTERESTS

I'm a quantitative macroeconomics working on family, public and labor economics. I'm particularly interested in understanding why being born to a particular family can shape so much one's opportunities, and in how public policy can generate and/or attenuate such dependence.
