I'm a PhD candidate in Economics at CEMFI since September 2015. I work under the supervision of Profs. Nezih Guner and Josep Pijoan-Mas. I will join NHH Norwegian School of Economics as an Assistant Professor in Sept. 2019.


RESEARCH INTERESTS

I'm a quantitative macroeconomics working on family, public and labor economics.

I'm particularly interested in understanding why being born to a particular family can shape so much one's opportunities, and in how public policy can generate and/or attenuate such dependence.
