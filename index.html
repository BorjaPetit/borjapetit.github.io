RESEARCH INTERESTS

Quantitative macro. Fiscal policy. Family economics. Read more

My research focuses on exploring why being born in a given family can shift one’s opportunities, and how/which policies can help increase equality of opportunities. More in particular, I study how public policies impact inequality and intergenerational mobility through their effects on family choices (fertility, investments in children's human capital, etc.) using heterogeneous agents models calibrated with microdata.
