CURRENT POSITION

I'm an analyst at the AIReF (Spanish Independent Authority for Fiscal Responsability) since Septemeber 2019. I'm in charge of the quantitative evaluation of tax benefits in Spain.

RESEARCH INTERESTS

Quantitative macro. Fiscal policy. Family economics. More

My research focuses on exploring why being born in a particular family can shift so much one’s opportunities, and how/which policies can help increasing equality of opportunities. More in particular, I study how public policies impact inequality and intergenerational mobility through their effects on family choices (fertility, investments in children's human capital, etc.) using quantitative macro models calibrated with microdata.

DOCTORAL STUDIES
